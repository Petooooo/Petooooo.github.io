## Question

## Model

Use inline math-style notation when needed, for example `E = mc^2`, and reserve full derivations for sections where they help the reader inspect assumptions.

## Reproducibility

Record environment, dataset, random seed, and code references. A research note should make future reconstruction easier, not just preserve a conclusion.
