## Question

Write the question in a form that can be checked later. Avoid turning a vague interest into a conclusion before there is a model or measurement.

## Model

Use notation when it reduces ambiguity. Keep assumptions close to the formula or algorithm they affect, and move longer derivations into their own section only when they help the reader audit the result.

## Reproducibility

Record environment, dataset, random seed, and code references. A research note should make future reconstruction easier, not just preserve a conclusion.
